Providing landscaping services with a smile.

For over 20 years ICE has created happy customers in the Twin Lakes, WI area. Our expertise and wide array of tools allow us to tackle any job both residential and commercial. We specialize in sports-field care.

Soil Retaining

In order to restrain soil to an unnatural slope, some form of soil retaining must be used. Whether it's a basic retaining wall - or a more custom solution - ICE provides the highest quality solutions for soil retaining.

De-icing & Salting

De-icing is the process of removing snow, ice or frost from a surface. We accomplish this by mechanical methods and by using products designed to lower the freezing point of water.

Weed & Fertilization

We offer extensive weed control which attempts to stop weeds, especially noxious or injurious weeds, from competing with desired flora and fauna. We also offer fertilization which enhances plant growth by providing essential nutrients.

Patio's

Installing a patio can be costly if done incorrectly. Building regulations, drainage, and grading can make installation a tedious process. Leave this intimidating process to a team of professionals with years of experience.
